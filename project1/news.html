SCHOLARSHIPS

Scholarships AUM offers a variety of scholarships, which is financial aid that doesn’t need to be repaid. Below, you’ll see scholarship opportunities for qualified: First-time incoming freshmen International students Community college transfer students ROTC & military students Currently enrolled undergraduate & graduate students Most scholarships require full-time attendance and call for other student responsibilities. To be considered for a scholarship, various criteria need to be met such as academic or extracurricular achievement and/or financial need. (To ensure that you are considered for all scholarship opportunities and student aid, please complete a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) annually. AUM’s Federal School Code is 008310.)
